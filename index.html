history

Moulind'Or, a well-known bakery and pastry brand, has humble beginnings rooted in tradition and quality. Established in the late 20th century, Moulind'Or started as a small family-owned bakery dedicated to crafting fresh, artisanal bread and pastries. Its name, which translates to "Golden Mill," reflects its commitment to excellence and the golden standard of its baked goods. Over the years, the brand expanded, introducing a diverse range of products while maintaining its focus on using high-quality ingredients and traditional techniques. Today, Moulind'Or is recognized for its fusion of authentic flavors and modern innovation, making it a beloved choice for bakery enthusiasts.
